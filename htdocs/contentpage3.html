Pollution: Causes and Solutions: Land Pollution
Land Pollution
As land-dwelling people, land pollution is likely the most obvious kinds of pollution in our eyes. It takes the form of mountains of trash and ruins the living spaces of humans and animals alike.
Causes of Land Pollution
  • Excessive production of non-biodegradable waste
  • Improper disposal of chemical waste

How to Help Limit Land Pollution
  • When possible, use reusable or biodegradable products instead of single-use, non-biodegradable things
  • Support businesses that are environmentally responsible
  • Support clean-up efforts
