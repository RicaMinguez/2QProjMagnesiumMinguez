Air Pollution
The air we breathe is, in many places, no longer as clean as it used to be. Fresh air has been ruined by the smoke of cars, factories, and cigarettes, but the smoke ruins more than just the air â€“ it ruins our lungs too.
Causes of Air Pollution
  • Smoke-belching vehicles
  • Smoke from factories
  • Smoke from cigarettes and large fires

How to Help Limit Air Pollution
  • Do not smoke cigarettes
  • Support businesses that use environmentally-friendly methods of production
  • Do not leave a flame unattended
  • Use public transportation instead of private vehicles when you can
  • If you own a car, keep it in good condition and have it repaired when needed such that it belches minimal to no smoke.
