Pollution: Causes and Solutions: Light Pollution
Light Pollution
Good lighting helps us stay safe and make the most of evenings. It is, in many cases, a necessary thing. However, the thousands of artificial lights on Earth, put together, are so bright that the sky in many places to never fully darken at night, a phenomenon called skyglow. This not only blocks out stars – it messes up the body clocks of animals, plants, and humans, potentially impacting their health.
Causes of Light Pollution
  • Outdoor light decorations
  • Light streaming out of open windows at night
  • Outdoor lamps

How to Help Limit Light Pollution
  • Avoid excessively bright lights
  • Use curtains and other coverings to direct light to only where it is needed
  • Turn off unneeded lights
  • Minimize outdoor lighting
  • Use warm light at night, when possible
