Pollution: Causes and Solutions: Light Pollution
Light Pollution
Good lighting helps us stay safe and make the most out of the evenings. However, the thousands of artificial lights on Earth are so bright that the sky in many places to never fully darken at night, a phenomenon called skyglow. This not only blocks out stars – it affects the body clocks and can impact the health of animals, plants, and humans.
Causes of Light Pollution
  • Outdoor light decorations
  • Light streaming out of open windows at night
  • Outdoor lamps

How to Help Limit Light Pollution
  • Use lights that are not excessively bright for their purpose
  • Use curtains and covered lights to keep light away from where it is unneeded
  • Turn off unneeded lights, especially if they are outdoors
  • Minimize outdoor lighting
  • Use warm light if it will also serve the needed purpose
