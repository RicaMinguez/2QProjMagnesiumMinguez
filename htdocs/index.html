Pollution: Causes and Solutions
Huge patches of plastic float in the oceans. Animals starve as they eat trash, thinking it is food. Oil spills taint the seas. Animals have begun to adapt to living among garbage that forms wide landfills and that peppers beaches. The Earth has so many problems, one of which is pollution, which is the contamination of the Earth. It may take different forms, such as smoke, plastic trash, toxic waste, oil spills, and even excessive light.
